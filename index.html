Unique benefits of living in Saudi Arabia

Choosing Saudi Arabia as your home offers a rewarding, enriching lifestyle backed by a tapestry of cultural, economic, and natural riches that cater to individuals and families alike.

Modern Infrastructure

Enjoy the convenience of modern cities equipped with advanced infrastructure, efficient transport systems, and cutting-edge amenities to boost productivity and comfort.

Dynamic Economy

Tap into a robust economy with diverse job prospects and a burgeoning market in tech and finance.

Hospitality and Warmth

Feel at home with the Saudi hospitality where everyone is welcomed and embraced, fostering a deep sense of community and belonging.

Culinary Delights

Savor the unique tastes of Saudi cuisine, blending traditional Arabian dishes with global culinary influences.

Family-Friendly Environment

Benefit from top-notch healthcare, educational systems, and a variety of family-oriented activities in safe, welcoming communities.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Delve into a vibrant cultural landscape featuring ancient landmarks, traditional festivals, and a thriving arts scene.

Natural Beauty

Experience Saudi Arabia's stunning natural environments from the serene Red Sea beaches to its expansive deserts and mountain ranges.

for Saudi Premium Residency

Passport Copies

Provide clear scanned copies of the passport for the main applicant and all family members included in the application.

Ensure that the passports are valid for a minimum of six months beyond the intended period of entry.

Personal Photos

Proof of Financial Capacity

Proof of financial solvency, like recent bank statements or a financial profile showing sufficient funds for living in Saudi Arabia, is required.
